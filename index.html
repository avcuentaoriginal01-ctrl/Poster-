Why is it dangerous?

Illegal trade has chain-reaction consequences that affect all of society.

💊

Health at Risk

Counterfeit medicines can make illnesses worse instead of curing them, putting lives in danger.

🏛️

Loss of Public Resources

Governments lose tax revenue, reducing funds available for schools, hospitals, and public services.

⚠️

Rising Crime

Illegal trade finances criminal networks and increases violence in vulnerable communities.

👶

Labor Exploitation

Many illegal supply chains rely on child labor and unsafe working conditions, violating basic human rights.

"A cheap product from an unknown source can cost you much more than you think."

Every irresponsible purchase feeds a chain of harm that affects real people: exploited workers, sick families, and communities left without resources.

What can we do?

Everyone has a role. From governments to citizens, the solution is collective.

01

Stronger Laws

Governments must create stricter legislation and improve controls at borders and markets.

02

Community Education

Communities can educate others about the risks of illegal products and promote responsible consumption.

03

Responsible Buying

Avoid cheap products from unknown sources. If it seems too good to be true, it probably is.

04

Report Suspicious Activity

If you see something, say something. Reporting illegal activity helps authorities act in time.
